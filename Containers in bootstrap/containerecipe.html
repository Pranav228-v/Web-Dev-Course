Hobby 1

One of my hobbies is cricket. its a game that I am pretty passionate about. I started playing cricket since 7. Some rules in cricket are as follows:

  • Two teams of 11 players compete on a field with a 22-yard pitch and a wicket at each end.
  • The batting team takes turns with the fielding team, batting and bowling. The bowler bowls the ball to the striker, who tries to hit it away from the wicket.
  • The batsmen try to score runs by crossing back and forth between the wickets. A run is scored each time both batsmen reach the opposite wicket. A ball hit over the boundary scores four points if it lands on the ground and six points if it's a fly ball.

These are just some of the rules in cricket and I'm barely scratching the surface! It's an exciting game that requires teamwork and excellent sportsmanship.
